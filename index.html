Welcome to Super Car Guys!

Save time, money and headaches. Come explore the largest used car inventory in the state of Kansas and shop with confidence knowing that your financing has already been approved.

Have no fear! Good or bad credit, our team of financing experts work directly with over 40 different lenders to get you the rates and terms you will be happy about. You’ll save thousands by buying used and you’ll never think about used cars the same way again.

We’re proud to offer great quality used cars in Wichita and the surrounding areas of Derby, Andover, Maize, Hutchinson, and Salina. Come visit any of our 3 Wichita locations and we look forward to the opportunity to serve you.
